Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 05, 2026

Welcome to Homizgo (“we”, “us”, “our”), a platform connecting users, landlords, and PG owners for property and PG rentals in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), DPDP Rules 2025, and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, password, and profile details.
  • Property & Listing Data: Property details, photos, location (Mumbai & other cities), rent, amenities.
  • Communication Data: Chat messages, emails, and support tickets.
  • Usage & Technical Data: IP address, device info, browser type, cookies, and analytics data.
  • Payment Data: (If applicable) Transaction details processed through secure third-party gateways.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your data only with your consent or for legitimate purposes under DPDP Act and GDPR, including:

  • Providing and improving our rental matching services.
  • Sending login notifications, chat alerts, and property approval updates.
  • Verifying identities and preventing fraud.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Data

We share data only as necessary:

  • With landlords/PG owners (only relevant property & contact info).
  • With service providers (payment gateways, email services like Resend, hosting partners) under strict contracts.
  • With government authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and optional cookies for analytics and personalization. See our Cookie Policy below.

5. Your Rights (DPDP & GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or erase your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Grievance redressal.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@homizgo.com. We respond within 30 days (or as per applicable law).

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, access controls, and regular audits to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes mentioned or as required by law.

8. International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in India. Any transfer outside India complies with DPDP and GDPR safeguards.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. You will be notified via email or in-app notice.
